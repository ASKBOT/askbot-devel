How It Works

Welcome to the Rover Q&A Community! We’re building connections between dog lovers, and creating a space for support and camaraderie.

From pet parents to sitters, we’re all united by a common love of dogs. At the Rover Q&A Community, our mission is to provide a space where dog lovers can connect with and learn from one another.

So feel free to take a look around, ask a question, or help others with your expertise. Our guidelines are simple: Be nice, be helpful, and celebrate our mutual love of dogs.
